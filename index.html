About Us

The Polytechnic University of the Philippines Quezon City Branch is a state university in Quezon City, Philippines. Established in 1997, it is one of over twenty branches among the PUP system. It offers quality education in the fields of information technology, marketing, entrepreneurship, management and more with its excellent faculty members, accomplished and expert in their respectful fields.

Our Programs

PUPQC offers quality education in several fields. The pride of the university is continuously producing top-notcher graduates every year.

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)
  • Bachelor of Business Technology and Livelihood Education major in Home Economics (BBTLEDHE)
  • Bachelor of Business Technology and Livelihood Education major in Information Communication and Technology (BTLEDICT)
  • Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Human Resource Management (BSBAHRM)
  • Bachelor of Science in Business Administration major in Marketing Management (BSBA-MM)
  • Bachelor of Science in Entrepreneurship (BSENTREP)
  • Bachelor of Public Administration major in Public Financial Management (BPAPFM)
  • Diploma in Office Management Technology Medical Office Management (DOMTMOM)

Mission

In consonance with the PUP Vision, the PUP GS commits itself to foster excellence in all key dimensions of graduate education: instruction, research, extension, community service, production, industry-academe partnerships, local and international linkages, and stakeholders empowerment.
